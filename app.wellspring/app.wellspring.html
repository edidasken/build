Why The Wellspring?

FlockOS was built to serve churches in 210 countries — including regions with unreliable internet, censored networks, or no connectivity at all. The gospel doesn't stop at the edge of a cellular tower.

The Wellspring enables 100% offline operation. Your entire database — members, families, sermons, prayer requests, everything — lives locally in your browser as a simple JSON file. No cloud required. No subscription fees. No tracking. No third-party access.

Built for Freedom & Privacy
  • Zero external dependencies: Native browser IndexedDB, no libraries, no bloat
  • Device-only storage: Your data never leaves your machine unless you export it
  • JSON format: Human-readable, portable, lightweight, and future-proof
  • Persecution-resistant: No cloud trail, no server logs, no API keys to compromise
  • Free forever: No hosting costs, no vendor lock-in, no surprise shutdowns

"The wind blows wherever it pleases. You hear its sound, but you cannot tell where it comes from or where it is going. So it is with everyone born of the Spirit." — John 3:8

The church must be able to operate independently, quietly, and resiliently — even when the internet goes dark. The Wellspring was built for that reality.
